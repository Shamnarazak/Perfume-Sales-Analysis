## Executive Summary

The luxury perfume industry is highly competitive, where understanding customer preferences, product performance, profitability, and regional sales trends is essential for sustainable business growth.

This interactive **Power BI dashboard** transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights by analyzing **revenue, profit, customer demographics, product performance, sales channels, branch performance, and purchasing behavior**.

The dashboard empowers business leaders to identify high-performing brands, optimize inventory, improve profitability, and make data-driven strategic decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Luxury retailers often collect large amounts of transactional data but struggle to answer critical business questions such as:

* Which perfume brands generate the highest revenue?
* Which products deliver the greatest profit?
* Which branches outperform others?
* How do customer demographics influence sales?
* Which sales channels perform better?
* How can profitability be increased?

Without interactive reporting, these insights remain difficult to uncover quickly.

## Key Insights

✔ Luxury brands such as **Ajmal** and **Dior Sauvage** generate the highest revenue.

✔ Monthly sales remain relatively stable with noticeable seasonal peaks.

✔ Revenue and profitability are not always directly proportional, emphasizing the importance of margin analysis.

✔ Local customers contribute the majority of overall revenue.

✔ Branch-level performance varies significantly, indicating opportunities for operational improvement.

✔ Average basket value differs across branches, providing insights for pricing and promotional strategies.

---

## Business Recommendations

✅ Prioritize inventory for top-selling brands.

✅ Increase marketing investment for high-margin products.

✅ Replicate successful sales strategies across lower-performing branches.

✅ Introduce personalized campaigns based on customer demographics.

✅ Monitor monthly revenue trends to improve inventory planning.

✅ Develop promotional offers targeting tourist segments during peak seasons.
